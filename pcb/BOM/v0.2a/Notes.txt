BILL OF MATERIALS (BOM)
----------------------

For one motorcycle, you will need the following parts:

- 2 x PCB boards

- 12 x 4 way Molex connectors (Plug) - Digikey part No: 0462071004-ND
- 12 x 4 way Molex connectors (Receptacle) - Digikey part No: WM13487-ND

- 44 x rectangular connectors - Digikey part No: 0039000038 (order more in case spares are needed)

- 44 x pins - Digikey part No: 5040-YJ1-17-0.52AU-ND (order more in case spares are needed)

Details are outlined in the spreadsheet found in this folder.
